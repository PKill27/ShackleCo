Choosing your soft shackle

Size

Our soft shackles are sized by measuring the long diameter while under tension.

Strength

We make soft shackles ranging in strength from 900 pounds to over 2000 of breaking strength.

Chafe Protection

For tough jobs you can get your soft shackles with a chafe gaurd for extended use(only for 5mm or longer)

Color

It is all up to you!! Color availability depends on the strenght of the shackle.

Endless Loops

Contact us directly, via the contact form bellow, with your specific needs for endless loops and we will work with you to find a solution.

Easy-open Tab

draw string to make opening your shackle easier

To order a custom shackle you should email us at Rigging@shackleco.com in the format below. You will recive an invoice for your order and upon payment, your order will be completed and shipped.
Email Format:

    Subject Line: Custom Shackle

    Email Body:
        Size: 1 inch minimum, no maximum
        Strength: 900/1600/2500/5400/8600/13,700/19,600/23,000(lbs) In cases that require it we can go up to over 100,000lbs of strength
        Chafe Protection: yes or no
        Color: Red, Green, Gray, Black, Blue(Depends on strength)
        EasyOpen: yes or no
        Type: Endless loop or soft shackle
