  • About Soft Shackles

    Soft shackles are a synthetic, floating alternative to metal shackles. Soft shackles made of Samson Amsteel rope are extreamly strong and durable
arrow_downward

Whatever your passion, pursue it with power. Our soft shackles deliver reliable, secure, and easy to use solutions to your challenge. In general, shackles serve as integral connection points between two metal pieces in many outdoor assemblies. Regardless of shape, shackles are identified by thickness, length, and how strong it is or load rating.
Metal shackles consistently get stronger as they get bigger making it easy to find the one you need however they can be hard to replace or take off in harsh weather, will corrode and get stuck, will sink if dropped in the water, and often have pins which aren't captive so if you drop the pin, you're going to have to go and find it.

Soft shackles exponentially get stronger as they get bigger so when in doubt, get a slightly larger one. Soft shackle ratings are more dependent on how long you've had it, the workmanship, and the method used to make it, however in general they are lighter, float in the water, consist of only one piece, easily come undone, and close firmly when under load.
